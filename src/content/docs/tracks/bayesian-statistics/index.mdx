title: Bayesian Statistics
---

This track builds Bayesian inference from first principles, starting with Bayes' rule and progressing through conjugate priors to full Bayesian linear regression.

## Notes

1. [Bayes Rule](./bayes-rule/) — the foundation of Bayesian inference
2. [Beta-Bernoulli Conjugate Prior](./beta-bernoulli-conjugate-prior/) — updating beliefs about a proportion
3. [Normal-Normal Conjugate Prior](./normal-normal-conjugate-prior/) — updating beliefs about a mean
4. [Bayesian Linear Regression](./bayesian-linear-regression/) — extending conjugacy to regression
5. [Multivariate Bayesian Linear Regression](./multivariate-bayesian-linear-regression/) — the full vector-coefficient case
